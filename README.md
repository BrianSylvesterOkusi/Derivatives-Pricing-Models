We explore the following models:
(1) Basic models - Black Scholes, CRR and Monte Carlo models
(2) Local Volatility models - Dupire and CEV models
(3) Stochastic models - Heston, Double Heston, SZ, Hull White and Jacobian models
(4) Local volatility stochastic/ hybrid models - Heston SLV, Double Heston SLV, CEV Hull White SLV, SZ SLV and Jacobian SLV models
(5) Jump & Diffusion Models - Merton's Jump-Diffusion, Bates, Kou’s Double Exponential and Affine Jump Diffusion models
(6) Option Pricing Models - Variance Gamma, General Exponential, Gaussian (Bachelier) models
(7) Interest Rate Models - Heath-Jarrow-Morton (HJM), Hull-White, LIBOR Market Model (LMM), SABR, Lognormal Short-Rate models
(8) Rough Derivative Pricing Models - Rough Heston, Rough Bergomi, Rough SABR, Forward Market Model (FMM) models
(9) Credit Models/ Copulas - Gaussian, Student-t, Multi-Factor Gaussian, Multi-Factor Clayton, Multi-Factor Gumbel models
(10) Commodity Pricing Models - One-Factor Mean-Reverting, Gibson-Schwartz, Schwartz-Smith, Seasonality-Adjusted, Jump-Diffusion models
